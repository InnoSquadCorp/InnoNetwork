# Internal Review Notes

Internal review and audit documents for InnoNetwork are not tracked in
this public repository. They live in a private archive maintained by the
`InnoSquadCorp` org.

Contributors and external reviewers do not need these notes — every
behavioural commitment is published in [API_STABILITY.md](../../API_STABILITY.md)
and [CHANGELOG.md](../../CHANGELOG.md), and architectural rationale lives
under `docs/` (see [docs/ClientArchitecture.md](../ClientArchitecture.md)
for the canonical entry point).

If you are a maintainer who needs the historical evaluation notes,
contact `@Ethan-IS`.
